MASTER SUPPLY AND SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Document Reference: SLA-ACME-2026-V2
Effective Date: March 15, 2026

PARTIES
This agreement is entered into by and between Global Manufacturing Inc. (hereinafter "Buyer") and Acme Materials Corp (hereinafter "Supplier").

DELIVERY AND LEAD TIMES
The Supplier agrees to fulfill all standard purchase orders within a strict maximum turnaround window. The standard delivery SLA lead time is established at exactly seventy-two (72) hours from the moment the electronic purchase order is acknowledged by the Supplier's ERP system.

LIQUIDATED DAMAGES FOR DELAY
Time is of the essence. In the event that the Supplier fails to deliver the materials within the aforementioned 72-hour window, the Supplier shall be liable for a delay penalty. The Buyer shall deduct a sum of $1,250.00 USD for each full calendar day the shipment is delayed beyond the agreed delivery date. Furthermore, for any individual units missing from the manifest upon arrival (short-shipments), a missed item penalty of $150.00 USD per missing unit shall be applied.

QUALITY ASSURANCE AND DEFECTS
The Supplier warrants that all raw materials will meet the strict chemical purity standards outlined in Appendix A. The minimum acceptable quality threshold is ninety-eight percent (98.0%) defect-free yield per batch. Should the quality score fall below this threshold, a quality penalty rate equal to fifteen percent (15%) of the total order value will be withheld by the Buyer.
