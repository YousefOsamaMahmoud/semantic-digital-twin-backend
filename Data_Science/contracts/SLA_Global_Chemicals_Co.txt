SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Document ID: SLA-GC-2026-101
Parties: The Manufacturer ("Buyer") and Global Chemicals Co. ("Supplier_Incumbent")
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
This agreement governs the supply of "Critical_Component" and "Material_X" to the Buyer's production facilities.
2. DELIVERY WINDOWS AND LEAD TIME
The Supplier agrees to a standard SLA Lead Time of forty-eight (48) hours from the moment a Purchase Order (PO) is issued. Deliveries arriving after this window will be classified as a Late Delivery.
3. PENALTY CLAUSES
3.1 Delay Penalties: In the event of a Late Delivery, the Supplier shall be subject to a Delay Penalty Rate of $500.00 USD per day of delay.
3.2 Missed Item Penalties: The Supplier must fulfill 100% of the Ordered Quantity. Any UnderShipment will incur a Missed Item Penalty Rate of $45.00 USD per missing unit.
3.3 Quality Thresholds: The Supplier guarantees that goods will arrive free of defect. The Minimum Quality Threshold is set at 0.95 (95% intact). If the Quality Score of a delivery falls below this threshold, it will be classified as Damaged Goods. The Quality Penalty Rate for such violations shall be calculated at 0.15 (15%) of the Total Order Cost.
4. TERMINATION
If the Supplier's Reliability Score falls below 0.50, the Buyer reserves the right to immediately terminate this agreement and route orders to an Alternative Supplier.
